Hugh Clowers Thompson Jr.

An aviator that stopped a massacre

  • He and his crewmates landed their helicopter between US soldiers and the villagers they were murdering
  • They held these US soldiers at gunpoint to stop the slaughter
  • The actions of Hugh Thompson and his crew successfully ended the Mỹ Lai Massacre
  • Thompson was later a primary witness against the murderers
  • Despite this they were initially ostracized for their actions while all participants in the killings were acquitted
